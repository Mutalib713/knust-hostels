About this hostel

Wagyingo Main Hostel is the flagship of Wagyingo's three KNUST properties, sitting at Ayeduase (Atta Mills Junction), about a 1.1 km walk to the Ayeduase gate. It's built around the promise of "comfort, security and academic excellence in modern accommodation."

Students consistently highlight its spacious rooms, strong security and quiet, "home away from home" feel. Rooms range from single through quad, plus dedicated study rooms, with Wi-Fi and a gaming area on site across its multiple floors.
