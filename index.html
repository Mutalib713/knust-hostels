How to book a hostel at KNUST

Accommodation works differently depending on your level. Here's the safe way to lock in a room — for freshers and continuing students alike.

🎓 Freshers (Level 100)

Most first-years are balloted into the traditional halls (Unity, Katanga, Republic, Queen's, Africa, Independence) through the university — apply early on the official portals below. Didn't get a hall, or want more comfort and privacy? A private hostel in Ayeduase, Kotei, Bomso or Gaza is the normal route. Rooms closest to campus fill up before the semester starts, so start searching now.

📚 Continuing students

From Level 200 onward you're mostly on your own — the halls keep only a limited number of continuing-student beds (online ballot). Almost everyone rents a private hostel or a self-contained room. If you liked your current place, renew early with the manager before others grab it; otherwise use the directory above to compare area, price and distance, then book ahead of the rush.
