How freshers can book a hostel

First-years (Level 100) are usually placed in traditional halls; most students use private hostels. Here's how to secure a room:

1. Official KNUST portals

Apply for on-campus halls / approved hostels via the university systems.

KOSASS ↗   KOHS ↗

2. Call the manager

Use the Call button on a hostel to ask about availability, price & viewing. Green tick = contact confirmed.

3. Visit before paying

Open Directions, go and inspect the room. Never pay before seeing it in person.

4. Booking platforms

Some hostels list rooms online with photos & prices.
